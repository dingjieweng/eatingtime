Content Sells

Snapask
Mobile - Web Design
Project Overview
Content Sells project is a project implemented in Snapask.
Snapask has to develop a new channel for students to fulfill the need besides the online tutoring, this project intends to develop a online learning-materials for the users.
Team
1 Growth Manager
1 Head of marketing
7 Local marketers
1 Designer
1 UX strategist
Responsibilities
Brand Design
Illustration
Lean UX
UI Design
Prototyping
Process
We are using Lean UX to minimize the waste. By cutting out the deliverables and documentation-based process we usually had, we are able to move rapidly and experiment things at a fast pace.
Hypothesis
We believe that creating the online learning contents,
For students who want more online resources,
Will get us a new channel for revenue.
We will know this is true when we see people buying these contents.

Design

As a Designer in the team, my goal is to came up with a low-effort but high quality page to present the contents we're selling. Since there's no engineers involved in the project, I decide to use a web page planted in our app so the idea could be tested easily.
Since the team members located in different regions, we discussed through video online meeting. The first version was produced after compiling the opinions of stakeholders.
I added 2 features to provide a better experience for users. Also followed the system of Snapask for design to keep the consistency.
  1. Author field: Increase users' trust in the content.
  2. Tags: Users can quickly recognize the relevance of content.
Visual Design
I designed a series of material for the Content Sells Project. They are being used as cover and advertising of the content. The elements are all study-related.
Feedbacks & Reflection
We released online contents in 7 regions for 2 month.
It shows Hong Kong, Taiwan and Japan's users are willing to pay.
Impact
We can see that although there are many competitors, users in some areas still have the willingness to pay. This validates the idea of developing online learning content and provides the next direction for the company's product development.
